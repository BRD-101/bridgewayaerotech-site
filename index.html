Transaction Advisory

Transaction support for aircraft and engine sales and leases, including contract negotiation, lease structuring, and closing coordination for investors, owners, airlines, and legal counsel.

Technical Due Diligence

Technical due diligence and documentation review covering airworthiness records, maintenance status, service bulletin compliance, and regulatory conformity to support informed transactional decisions.

Fleet Strategy Advisory

Independent fleet planning and strategy analysis for airlines, operators, and investors, covering acquisition, retirement, conversion, and lifecycle planning across aircraft and engine types.

Asset Management & Inspections

About Us

Independent expertise.
Disciplined execution.

Bridgeway Aero Tech's team is comprised of seasoned aviation professionals specializing in aircraft acquisition, leasing, and regulatory compliance. We partner closely with clients to provide pragmatic, efficient solutions that reduce risk and enable seamless aircraft integration.

We serve investors, owners, airlines, and legal counsel by delivering clear, disciplined insight at the intersection of engineering, regulation, and commercial aviation — ensuring informed decisions, seamless execution, and reliable outcomes in complex aviation transactions.

2005

Founded as TB Aviation Services

Established to provide independent, hands-on technical support to aircraft leasing companies without in-house technical teams, specializing in physical inspections, asset recovery, and technical evaluations designed to protect asset condition and ensure lease and regulatory compliance.

2024

Rebranded as Bridgeway Aero Tech

Expanded services beyond technical oversight to include aviation transaction advisory, asset management, remarketing, and aviation-specific support for investors and legal counsel. Today, Bridgeway Aero Tech delivers disciplined, independent expertise across the full aviation asset lifecycle, helping clients reduce risk and protect capital where engineering realities meet commercial and legal outcomes.
